About Us

About Dubey Nursing Home

Dubey Nursing Home is a trusted healthcare center situated on Deoghar Road, Jamua. The hospital is dedicated to providing compassionate, affordable and quality healthcare services with experienced doctors, trained staff and modern medical facilities.

Founded and led by Dr. Rajesh Kumar Dubey (Owner) alongside Dr. Rina Dubey (Director), our hospital reflects a family's long-standing commitment to the health and well-being of the Jamua community.

Our Mission

To provide reliable healthcare services with patient comfort and safety as our highest priority.
